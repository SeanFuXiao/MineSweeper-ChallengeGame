Mine Sweeper

How to Play

- Left-Click  to reveal a cell.
- Right-Click to flag a cell as a mine.
- Both-Click  to reveal surrounding cells.
- Win Condition:  Flag all mines.   🏴‍☠️🏴‍☠️🏴‍☠️
- Lose Condition: Trigger a Mines.  💥💥💥
